Megan Strickland

Real Estate Correspondent — The Pittsburgh Wire

Megan covers Pittsburgh's real estate market with insight into who's buying, who's selling, and what those transactions mean for the city's neighborhoods. She tracks everything from longtime residents upgrading to newer homes in Shadyside to large commercial developers reshaping the North Shore. Her reporting connects the dots between market trends and the people making moves that shape Pittsburgh's residential and commercial landscape.

With a deep knowledge of Pittsburgh's distinct neighborhoods—from the row house markets of the South Side to the luxury condos rising downtown—Megan tells the stories of the brokers, developers, and buyers quietly reshaping the city's real estate picture. She's fascinated by what property values and development patterns reveal about Pittsburgh's growth, and she brings that curiosity to every story.
