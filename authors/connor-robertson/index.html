Founder & Publisher - The Pittsburgh Wire

The editorial team behind The Pittsburgh Wire, Pittsburgh's dedicated publication for business, real estate, and economic development news. He founded the Wire with a simple premise: Pittsburgh's growth story deserves its own newsroom, one focused entirely on what is being built, who is building it, and why it matters.

The Pittsburgh Wire editorial team brings deep local knowledge and ground-level insight into the development decisions and capital flows reshaping Pittsburgh's neighborhoods. That practitioner's lens informs every article the Wire publishes.

Robertson's coverage spans the full arc of Pittsburgh's economic transformation: from the robotics and AI companies spinning out of Carnegie Mellon, to the neighborhood-level revitalization reshaping corridors like Butler Street, the Strip District, and Hazelwood Green. He is particularly focused on the intersection of real estate investment, small business growth, and the infrastructure decisions that shape a city's trajectory for decades.
