Connor Robertson

Publisher & Editor — The Pittsburgh Wire

Connor Robertson founded The Pittsburgh Wire to give the city's business community, investors, and residents a dedicated publication for Pittsburgh's growth story. Under his editorial direction, the Wire covers business openings, real estate development, infrastructure investment, and the people driving Pittsburgh's forward momentum.

A longtime observer of Pittsburgh's economic transformation, Robertson believes the city's most compelling chapters are still being written — in the Strip District, in Hazelwood, on the North Shore, and in every neighborhood where an entrepreneur is betting on Pittsburgh's future.
