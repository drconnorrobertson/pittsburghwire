Patrick Kaminski

Development & Construction Correspondent — The Pittsburgh Wire

Patrick covers Pittsburgh's most visible transformation—the cranes, the groundbreakings, the ribbon cuttings that mark the city's physical growth. He follows major projects from planning through completion, interviewing the developers, architects, and contractors shaping Pittsburgh's skyline and neighborhoods. His reporting reveals how development decisions ripple through communities, changing not just the built environment but the character of the neighborhoods around them.

Whether it's a new bridge, a revitalized warehouse district, or a major infrastructure project, Patrick brings clarity to Pittsburgh's construction pipeline. He's interested in the people making decisions, the financing behind big projects, and what completed developments mean for the residents and businesses that live alongside them. For Patrick, every crane tells a story about Pittsburgh's confidence in its future.
