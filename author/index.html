About The Founder

Dr. Connor Robertson

Dr. Connor Robertson is a Pittsburgh-based entrepreneur, author, and business strategist. He founded The Pittsburgh Wire to spotlight the people, businesses, and developments shaping the city's future.

With a background spanning business strategy, real estate development, and entrepreneurship, Dr. Robertson brings a unique perspective to Pittsburgh's business landscape. His passion for the city's potential—rooted in both economic opportunity and community pride—drives The Pittsburgh Wire's mission to celebrate the builders, founders, and visionaries transforming Pittsburgh.

Dr. Robertson is committed to telling Pittsburgh's forward-looking stories: the entrepreneurs launching ambitious ventures, the developers betting on neighborhoods, the real estate investors who see what's next, and the professionals choosing to build their careers in this remarkable city. He believes Pittsburgh's greatest competitive advantage is its authenticity, its people, and its relentless optimism.

Beyond The Pittsburgh Wire, Dr. Robertson is an active voice in Pittsburgh's business community, sharing insights on entrepreneurship, strategy, and the city's evolving economy across multiple platforms and publications.
