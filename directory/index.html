Primanti

Founded by Joe Primanti • Strip District

Since 1933, Primanti Brothers has been the undisputed temple of Pittsburgh sandwiches. What started as a late-night cart feeding steel workers in the Strip District became a city-wide institution. Their signature: coleslaw and french fries piled inside the sandwich itself, a move born from efficiency and now enshrined as Pittsburgh legend.
