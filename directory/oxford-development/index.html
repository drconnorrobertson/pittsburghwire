The Story

Oxford Development was founded in 1955 by the Lewis family and has grown into one of the most respected commercial real estate developers in western Pennsylvania. The firm's portfolio includes millions of square feet of office, retail, and mixed-use space across the Pittsburgh region.

Oxford's developments include some of Pittsburgh's most recognizable commercial properties. The firm takes a vertically integrated approach, handling development, construction, leasing, and property management in-house, which gives them control over every aspect of their projects.

Now led by the second generation of the Lewis family, Oxford continues to develop major projects that shape Pittsburgh's skyline and commercial landscape.

What Makes It Pittsburgh

Oxford Development has been building Pittsburgh for nearly 70 years. The Lewis family's commitment to the city through multiple economic cycles demonstrates the kind of long-term thinking that has always defined Pittsburgh's best business families.

The firm's Downtown and suburban developments provide the commercial infrastructure that supports thousands of Pittsburgh jobs and businesses.
