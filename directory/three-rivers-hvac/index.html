The Story

Patrick Sullivan started Three Rivers HVAC in 2006 with one truck and a commitment to transparent pricing and quality work. What began as a one-person operation doing residential repair calls has grown into Pittsburgh's most trusted HVAC contractor for both residential and commercial customers. The company operates across the city and surrounding region, handling everything from routine maintenance to complex commercial installations.

The name reflects Pittsburgh's geography—the confluence of three rivers at the city's heart. Three Rivers HVAC employs 34 licensed technicians and maintains service agreements with hundreds of commercial properties, from office buildings to manufacturing facilities. Residential customers appreciate the transparent pricing and same-day service availability. The company uses state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment and stays current with the latest energy-efficient systems, helping customers reduce their carbon footprint and utility costs.

Three Rivers has completed over 12,000 installations and maintains a 96% customer satisfaction rating based on verified reviews. The company invests heavily in technician training and certification, recognizing that HVAC work requires both technical expertise and customer service skills.

What Makes It Pittsburgh

Pittsburgh winters are cold and unpredictable. When your heat goes out in January, you need someone local who will show up quickly and fix it right. Three Rivers HVAC has built its reputation on that reliability—being the people Pittsburgh turns to when the temperature drops.

Patrick remains actively involved in the business and maintains the culture of craftsmanship and customer care that started with one truck twenty years ago.
