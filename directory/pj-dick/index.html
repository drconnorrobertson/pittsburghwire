The Story

PJ Dick Incorporated was founded in 1979 and has grown into a major Pittsburgh-based general contractor. Part of the Massaro Corporation family of construction companies, PJ Dick has become known for building many of Pittsburgh's most iconic buildings and infrastructure projects.

The company employs over 500 people and generates more than $1 billion in revenue, making it one of the region's largest construction firms. PJ Dick's portfolio spans healthcare facilities, educational buildings, commercial developments, and complex infrastructure projects. The company's reputation for quality, reliability, and innovation has made it a trusted partner for major clients.

PJ Dick's growth reflects Pittsburgh's construction sector: a tradition of taking on complex projects, delivering on time and on budget, and building structures that last. The company has been integral to Pittsburgh's modern development, leaving its mark on the city's skyline and infrastructure.

What Makes It Pittsburgh

Pittsburgh's identity is shaped by builders and engineers who take pride in their work. PJ Dick represents that tradition: a company that builds things to last, that takes on challenges others might avoid, and that invests in the community it serves.

The construction industry is a backbone of Pittsburgh's economy, and companies like PJ Dick demonstrate that Pittsburgh can build anything. That confidence—that we can solve problems, build quality projects, and contribute to the region's growth—is deeply Pittsburgh.
