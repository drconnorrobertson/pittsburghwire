The Story

For generations of Pittsburgh families, S.W. Randall Toyes & Giftes has been the place where birthday presents are found, holiday lists are filled, and children's eyes go wide. The family-owned specialty retailer has been a fixture in downtown Pittsburgh for over 45 years, occupying its charming storefront on Smithfield Street.

The store carries an extensive selection of toys, games, puzzles, and gifts for all ages -- from classic wooden toys and board games to the latest trending items. What makes S.W. Randall special is the personal touch: staff who know their inventory, gift wrapping that makes every purchase feel special, and an atmosphere that turns shopping into an experience.

In an era when most toy shopping has moved online, S.W. Randall thrives because it offers something Amazon cannot -- discovery, delight, and human connection.

What Makes It Pittsburgh

Downtown Pittsburgh has seen many retailers come and go, but S.W. Randall endures because Pittsburgh families pass it down like a tradition. Parents who shopped there as children now bring their own kids, making it one of the city's most enduring retail institutions.
