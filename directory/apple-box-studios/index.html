The Story

Apple Box Studios was founded in 2004 as a full-service marketing agency specializing in healthcare, technology, and medtech sectors. With 25+ employees based in Pittsburgh's Strip District, the agency delivers integrated campaigns that span strategy, brand development, digital, and production.

Apple Box has carved out a niche in the healthcare and life sciences marketing space, working with medical device companies, biotech firms, and healthcare providers. The agency's deep expertise in medtech marketing reflects Pittsburgh's growing reputation as a center for health innovation and life sciences entrepreneurship. From concept to execution, Apple Box supports Pittsburgh's innovation ecosystem through strategic communications.

The agency's growth and client success demonstrate the strength of Pittsburgh's creative sector and the city's ability to support specialized agencies that serve emerging industries like healthcare technology and innovation.

What Makes It Pittsburgh

Apple Box Studios is part of Pittsburgh's expanding creative and medtech ecosystem. Located in the Strip District, an area undergoing revitalization as a hub for creative businesses and startups, the agency benefits from and contributes to the neighborhood's transformation. The agency's healthcare specialization connects it to Pittsburgh's leadership in healthcare innovation and medical research.

Pittsburgh's position as a center for healthcare, robotics, and AI innovation creates demand for agencies that understand these technical sectors. Apple Box's success in medtech marketing reflects Pittsburgh's distinctive advantage as a city where creative talent meets technical expertise in life sciences and healthcare.
