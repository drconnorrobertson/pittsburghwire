The Story

Howard Hanna Real Estate Services began with a single office in Shadyside in 1957, founded by Howard W. Hanna Jr. and Anne Freyvogel Hanna. Today, it is the #1 family-owned and -operated real estate company in the United States, and the largest privately-owned real estate firm nationally with $6.2 billion in managed assets.

The company offers residential and commercial real estate sales, property management, and mortgage services across the Mid-Atlantic region. Despite its massive growth, Howard Hanna has remained headquartered in Pittsburgh and family-led, with the next generation of the Hanna family continuing the legacy.

From its origins on Centre Avenue in Shadyside, Howard Hanna has grown to serve buyers and sellers across Pennsylvania, West Virginia, Ohio, and surrounding states, while maintaining the personal touch and community commitment that defined it from day one.

What Makes It Pittsburgh

Pittsburgh is a city that values loyalty and local roots. Howard Hanna could have moved its headquarters to any major market, but it has stayed in Pittsburgh -- a decision that reflects the company's values and its connection to the community that built it.
