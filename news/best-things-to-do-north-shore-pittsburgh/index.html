Best Things to Do in North Shore

The 12 best things to do in Pittsburgh's North Shore. Sports, museums, parks, and entertainment across the river from downtown.

By The Pittsburgh Wire May 5, 2026 6 min read

The North Shore sits across the Allegheny River from downtown, connected by landmark bridges and packed with iconic attractions. Professional sports stadiums, world-class museums, a casino, and miles of riverfront trail create a neighborhood built for entertainment. These twelve experiences capture the best of Pittsburgh's North Shore.

1. PNC Park

North Shore • 115 Federal St, Pittsburgh, PA 15212

Among America's best ballparks with intimate sightlines, downtown skyline views, and legendary pierogi races.

2. Andy Warhol Museum

North Shore • 117 Sandusky St, Pittsburgh, PA 15212

World's largest single-artist museum with seven floors of Warhol's paintings, prints, films, and archives.

3. Acrisure Stadium

North Shore • 100 Art Rooney Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15212

Home of the Steelers and 2026 NFL Draft host. Year-round tours including field, locker rooms, and Hall of Honors.

4. National Aviary

North Shore • 700 Arch St, Pittsburgh, PA 15212

Only independent indoor nonprofit aviary in the US with 500+ birds and walk-through habitats.

5. Children's Museum

North Shore • 10 Children's Way, Pittsburgh, PA 15212

Named best children's museum in America. Imaginative play spaces, maker shop, and outdoor Backyard area.

6. Randyland

North Shore • 1501 Arch St, Pittsburgh, PA 15212

Free open-air art covering an entire house with vibrant color and folk art. Pittsburgh's most photographed spot.

7. Mattress Factory

North Shore • 500 Sampsonia Way, Pittsburgh, PA 15212

Contemporary art museum with site-specific installations transforming rooms into immersive experiences.

8. North Shore Riverfront Park

North Shore • N Shore Dr, Pittsburgh, PA 15212

Green spaces, public art, and river trail with stunning downtown views across the water.

9. Rivers Casino

North Shore • 777 Casino Dr, Pittsburgh, PA 15212

Full-service casino with gaming, restaurants, live entertainment, and regular concerts.

10. Bicycle Heaven

North Shore • 1800 Preble Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15233

World's largest bicycle museum with 3,500 bikes on display. Free, eccentric, and uniquely Pittsburgh.

11. Kayak Pittsburgh

North Shore • N Shore Dr, Pittsburgh, PA 15212

Rent kayaks to paddle beneath bridges with the skyline above. No experience required.

12. Kamin Science Center

North Shore • 1 Allegheny Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15212

Interactive exhibits, planetarium, submarine tour, and SportsWorks gallery. Top family destination.
