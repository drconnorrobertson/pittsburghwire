Best Hair Salons in Pittsburgh

The 12 best hair salons in Pittsburgh for 2026. Top-rated stylists for cuts, color, balayage, and extensions in Shadyside and Lawrenceville.

By The Pittsburgh Wire May 5, 2026 6 min read

Pittsburgh's salon scene goes far beyond basic cuts and color. From globally recognized colorists to neighborhood barbershops with decades of history, the city's best hair professionals combine artistry with genuine care for their clients. Whether you need a precision balayage, a bold transformation, or just a reliable stylist who gets your hair, these twelve salons consistently deliver exceptional results.

1. Whitehouse Salon

Shadyside • 5523 Walnut St, Pittsburgh, PA 15232

A fashion-forward boutique in the heart of Shadyside's style district focused on customer service. Stylists like Flo, Tanya, and Darius deliver trendy cuts and color matching each client's personal style.

2. Craft Collective Salon Group

Squirrel Hill • 5844 Forbes Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15217

Led by globally recognized Wella Professionals artist Derek Piekarski, specializing in highlights, balayage, blonding, and dimensional color with editorial-level technique.

3. Salon Bloom Color and Co.

Lawrenceville • 4614 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

With a perfect 5.0 rating across thousands of reviews, Salon Bloom excels at everything from natural-looking highlights to vivid fashion colors in a welcoming space.

4. Umi Hair Salon

Lawrenceville • 4213 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

The friendliest and most talented team in Pittsburgh. Umi delivers consistent, high-quality cuts and color with particular expertise in Asian hair and precision cutting.

5. Alora Salon & Wellness Spa

Wexford • 10529 Perry Hwy, Wexford, PA 15090

A 5.0-rated salon and spa combining exceptional hair services with full wellness offerings. From balayage to keratin treatments to relaxing spa services.

6. Alchemy Dry Cut Lounge

Shadyside • 5507 Walnut St, Pittsburgh, PA 15232

Specializing in dry cutting technique for more precise, longer-lasting shapes. The Shadyside lounge atmosphere is relaxed and creative.

7. Pageboy Salon & Boutique

Lawrenceville • 4724 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Expert hair services blended with a curated retail boutique offering independent beauty and lifestyle brands. Creative cuts, vivid color, and editorial styling.

8. Mister Grooming & Goods

Lawrenceville • 5020 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

A modern barbershop offering precision cuts, beard trims, hot towel shaves, and curated grooming products. Classic barbershop feel updated with contemporary style.

9. Heart of Glass

Bloomfield • 4106 Penn Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

Known for bold, creative color work and a welcoming, inclusive atmosphere. Vivid fantasy colors and perfectly blended lived-in looks.

10. Veraldi Salon

Squirrel Hill • 2020 Murray Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15217

A Squirrel Hill institution offering full-service hair care. Classic cuts to complex color correction with long-term client relationships built on consistency and expertise.

11. Cardamone's Salon

Bloomfield • 4703 Liberty Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

A Bloomfield neighborhood salon with deep community roots. Reliable, high-quality cuts and color in a warm, family-run atmosphere with affordable pricing.

12. Lisa Marie Salon

Ross Township • 4727 McKnight Rd, Pittsburgh, PA 15237

A top-rated North Hills salon known for precision cutting and color expertise. Polished, sophisticated results from corporate-ready blowouts to special occasion styling.
