Best Real Estate Developments in Pittsburgh

The 12 biggest real estate developments in Pittsburgh for 2026. From the $740M Esplanade to UPMC's hospital tower and downtown conversions.

By The Pittsburgh Wire May 5, 2026 8 min read

Pittsburgh is in the midst of a construction and development boom reshaping entire neighborhoods. From massive mixed-use projects on former industrial sites to the adaptive reuse of historic downtown office buildings, billions of dollars in investment are transforming the city's physical landscape. These twelve developments represent the most significant real estate projects shaping Pittsburgh in 2026 and beyond.

1. The Esplanade at Chateau

North Side • Chateau St, Pittsburgh, PA 15233

Piatt Companies broke ground on this $740 million, 15-acre mixed-use development in late 2025. The former brownfield near Rivers Casino will include 750 housing units, a 126-unit condo, retail, a hotel, an amphitheater, and a 180-foot Ferris wheel.

2. UPMC Kamin Hospital Tower

Oakland • 200 Lothrop St, Pittsburgh, PA 15213

UPMC's $1.3 billion, 17-story hospital tower in Oakland is one of the region's largest projects. The 636-bed facility will anchor transplant, cardiology, and neurology care when it opens in January 2027.

3. Gulf Tower Hotel Bardo

Downtown • 707 Grant St, Pittsburgh, PA 15219

The iconic 44-story Art Deco Gulf Tower is being converted into a boutique hotel. The $100 million adaptive reuse project preserves the building's landmark crown while bringing new hospitality energy to downtown.

4. Hazelwood Green

Hazelwood • 4800 Lytle St, Pittsburgh, PA 15207

The 178-acre former steel mill site continues its transformation with new residential, office, and retail. Tishman Speyer expects 3,000 to 4,000 total housing units at full buildout.

5. Arts Landing

Cultural District • Eighth St, Pittsburgh, PA 15222

The Pittsburgh Cultural Trust's 4-acre park features a central lawn, bandshell, pickleball courts, and welcome center. Opened ahead of the 2026 NFL Draft, transforming underused land into vibrant public space.

6. Pittsburgh International Airport Terminal

Findlay Township • 1000 Airport Blvd, Pittsburgh, PA 15231

The $1.7 billion landside terminal opened in late 2025. The 811,000-square-foot facility doubles security capacity and brings a modern arrival experience to the region.

7. Uptown Flats

Uptown • 1801 Fifth Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15219

A $22.8 million affordable housing development providing 34 family units focused on households earning 30% or below area median income. Opened spring 2026.

8. South Side Works Phase Three

South Side • 2743 E Carson St, Pittsburgh, PA 15203

The next phase at the former LTV Steel site brings additional office, residential, and retail to the South Side riverfront. New tech tenants are filling the mixed-use complex.

9. Lower Hill FNB Financial Center

Hill District • 1000 Fort Duquesne Blvd, Pittsburgh, PA 15222

First National Bank's new regional headquarters anchors the ongoing Lower Hill redevelopment, reconnecting the Hill District to downtown after decades of urban renewal damage.

10. City Edge Apartments

South Side • 50 S 27th St, Pittsburgh, PA 15203

A new luxury apartment development on the South Side riverfront offering modern units with panoramic city views in one of Pittsburgh's most walkable neighborhoods.

11. Bedford Dwellings Phase Two

Hill District • 2465 Bedford Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15219

The second phase brings new affordable and mixed-income housing to the Hill District, replacing aging public housing with modern, energy-efficient units.

12. Piatt Esplanade Residential

Chateau • 400 Beaver Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15233

Part of the larger Esplanade development, delivering luxury condos and apartments with river views, transforming Pittsburgh's Chateau neighborhood into a vibrant waterfront community.
