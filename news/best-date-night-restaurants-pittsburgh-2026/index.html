Best Date Night Restaurants in Pittsburgh

The 12 best date night restaurants in Pittsburgh for 2026. Romantic dining from Mount Washington views to intimate Lawrenceville bistros.

By The Pittsburgh Wire May 5, 2026 7 min read

Pittsburgh offers no shortage of restaurants that set the mood for a memorable evening. From mountaintop dining rooms with sweeping skyline views to intimate neighborhood spots with candlelit tables and thoughtful wine lists, the city's romantic restaurant scene covers every price point and style. These twelve restaurants deliver the atmosphere, food, and service that make an evening feel special.

1. Altius

Mount Washington • 1230 Grandview Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15211

Fine dining with the most spectacular view in the city. Contemporary American cuisine with panoramic vistas of the Pittsburgh skyline. Request a window table at sunset for the full effect.

2. LeMont Restaurant

Mount Washington • 1114 Grandview Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15211

Synonymous with elegance since 1960. White-tablecloth atmosphere, impeccable service, and classic continental menu. Pittsburgh's quintessential special occasion restaurant.

3. Poulet Bleu

Lawrenceville • 5104 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Parisian-inspired ambiance with sophisticated French menu and one of the city's most impressive bar programs. Intimate dining room with warm lighting and attentive service.

4. Eleven

Strip District • 1150 Smallman St, Pittsburgh, PA 15222

Contemporary American fine dining with elegant multi-level space, polished service, and refined seasonal menus. Rooftop bar perfect for pre-dinner cocktails.

5. Alla Famiglia

South Side • 804 E Warrington Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15210

Family-owned fine dining Italian with homemade pasta, award-winning wine list, and handcrafted cocktails. Intimate and genuinely warm.

6. Morcilla

Lawrenceville • 3519 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Spanish-inspired small plates meant for sharing. Intimate space with dim lighting and a parade of pintxos. Adventurous and intimate simultaneously.

7. Con Alma

Cultural District • 500 Penn Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15222

Jazz club and restaurant where live music sets the mood every evening. Excellent cocktails, refined small plates, and world-class jazz.

8. Balvanera

Strip District • 18 21st St, Pittsburgh, PA 15222

Argentinian-inspired cuisine with dramatic presentation, wood-fired dishes, extensive South American wine list, and moody atmosphere.

9. Girasole

Shadyside • 733 Copeland St, Pittsburgh, PA 15232

Refined northern Italian cuisine in an intimate, candlelit Shadyside setting. Handmade pastas, seasonal risottos, and curated Italian wines.

10. Pusadee's Garden

Lawrenceville • 5321 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Authentic Thai cuisine in a stunning garden setting. String lights, tropical plants, and gentle intimacy create one of Pittsburgh's most romantic environments.

11. Lorelei

East Liberty • 124 S Highland Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

Moody cocktail bar with creative drinks, thoughtful small plates, and vintage aesthetic. Strong first-date choice.

12. The Forge

East Liberty • 6025 Penn Circle S, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

Sleek restaurant with industrial-chic interior, creative seasonal menu, and one of the best cocktail programs in the city.
