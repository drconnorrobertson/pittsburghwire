Moonlit Burgers Opens Fourth Location in Garfield, Anchoring Penn Avenue Corridor

The smash burger pop-up becomes a full restaurant operator, with a kitchen-equipped fourth location in Garfield.

By Megan Strickland January 12, 2026 4 min read

Moonlit Burgers expands to a fourth Pittsburgh location // Photo: Unsplash

Moonlit Burgers has moved from pop-up to full restaurant. The smash burger outfit, which built a following through its mobile kitchen and temporary residencies at neighborhood bars, has opened its fourth and largest location on Penn Avenue in Garfield, with a proper kitchen, full table service, and plans to become a permanent fixture of the Penn Avenue corridor.

The expansion marks a turning point for the business. Moonlit Burgers started as a one-truck operation, rolling into parking lots and bar patios, building a customer base through word of mouth and social media. Over three years, the concept has proven itself: there is durable demand for smash burgers done well, and Pittsburghers will seek them out.

Moonlit's Growth
4 Total locations now operating, with Garfield as the first with a permanent kitchen
2,000+ Square feet of kitchen and dining space in the new Garfield restaurant
Penn Ave The corridor where Moonlit is anchoring its first permanent neighborhood location

Penn Avenue Gets Its Burger Anchor

Penn Avenue in Garfield has been in transition for years. The corridor is anchored by Garfield Jubilee, a community organization that has been working to strengthen the neighborhood through programming, business development, and neighborhood activation. Moonlit Burgers is the kind of tenant the avenue needs: a brand people will travel for, but rooted in the neighborhood's future.

"We wanted a location where we could build something permanent, not just pass through."

Moonlit Burgers Ownership

The Garfield location includes a full kitchen, counter seating, and table service for the first time. The menu is expanded beyond the pop-up's core smash burger offering, but the principle is the same: quality meat, proper technique, minimal embellishment. It is a formula that has worked at every temporary location. The question has always been whether it would work with a permanent space. Garfield is about to find out.

The Bloomfield-Garfield Corporation has been actively recruiting restaurant operators to the corridor. Moonlit Burgers is proof that the strategy is working. A restaurant that could have opened anywhere chose to open here.
