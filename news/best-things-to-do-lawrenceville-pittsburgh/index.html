Best Things to Do in Lawrenceville

The 12 best things to do in Lawrenceville, Pittsburgh. Restaurants, breweries, shopping, and entertainment on Butler Street.

By The Pittsburgh Wire May 5, 2026 7 min read

Lawrenceville has transformed from a working-class neighborhood into Pittsburgh's most dynamic cultural corridor while maintaining its gritty authenticity. Stretching along Butler Street from 34th to 62nd, the neighborhood offers an unmatched concentration of independent restaurants, craft breweries, boutique shops, and creative spaces. These twelve spots capture what makes Lawrenceville essential.

1. Row House Cinema

Lawrenceville • 4115 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

A single-screen theater showing curated weekly programming. One week might be 90s horror, the next Miyazaki retrospectives. Full bar and community vibe.

2. Arsenal Lanes

Lawrenceville • 212 44th St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

A retro bowling alley doubling as a weekend nightlife destination. Cosmic bowling, full bar, and fun atmosphere for families by day and groups at night.

3. Pusadee's Garden

Lawrenceville • 5321 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Authentic Thai cuisine in a gorgeous garden patio. Dishes like khao soi and green curry transport you to northern Thailand.

4. Market House

Lawrenceville • 4548 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Eclectic boutique shops including fine art at The Waverly Press, plants at Camouflage, and sustainable fashion at Fjord & Fable.

5. Hop Farm Brewing

Lawrenceville • 5601 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Spacious brewery with large taproom and outdoor area. Rotating food pop-ups and hop-forward beers make it a natural gathering spot.

6. Roberto Clemente Museum

Lawrenceville • 3339 Penn Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

The world's largest collection of Clemente memorabilia. Tours cover his remarkable career and humanitarian legacy.

7. Piccolo Forno

Lawrenceville • 3801 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Authentic Italian wood-fired pizza and pasta. Imported ingredients, reliable quality, and homemade gelato.

8. Spirit

Lawrenceville • 242 51st St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Part music venue, part bar, part pizza shop. Live shows from underground electronic to indie rock with legitimately great pizza.

9. Church Brew Works

Lawrenceville • 3525 Liberty Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Craft brewery in a restored 1902 Catholic church. Brewing kettles where the altar stood, stained glass, and award-winning beers.

10. Pageboy Salon & Boutique

Lawrenceville • 4724 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Expert hair services alongside independent beauty and lifestyle brands. Creativity and craftsmanship combined.

11. Morcilla

Lawrenceville • 3519 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Spanish pintxos and charcuterie with unmatched sherry selection. Barcelona tapas bar energy on Butler Street.

12. Wildcard

Lawrenceville • 4801 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Creative cocktail bar with inventive drinks, rotating art, and a vibe that changes with each visit.
