Best Things to Do in Squirrel Hill

The 12 best things to do in Squirrel Hill, Pittsburgh. Restaurants, parks, independent shops, and culture along Murray and Forbes Avenues.

By The Pittsburgh Wire May 5, 2026 6 min read

Squirrel Hill is Pittsburgh's most walkable and culturally diverse neighborhood. Two thriving commercial corridors along Murray Avenue and Forbes Avenue offer everything from world-class Asian cuisine to independent bookstores to one of the city's last remaining single-screen cinemas. These twelve spots capture what makes Squirrel Hill essential.

1. Frick Park

Squirrel Hill • 2005 Beechwood Blvd, Pittsburgh, PA 15217

Pittsburgh's largest park with 644 acres of trails. Blue Slide Park, made famous by Mac Miller, draws families.

2. The Manor Theatre

Squirrel Hill • 1729 Murray Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15217

Independent cinema showing art house and foreign films since 1939. Movies you cannot see at the multiplex.

3. Mineo's Pizza House

Squirrel Hill • 2128 Murray Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15217

A legend since 1958. Thick Pittsburgh-style pizza with chewy crust, tangy sauce, and generous cheese.

4. Jerry's Records

Squirrel Hill • 2136 Murray Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15217

Over a million LPs in one of the country's largest independent record stores. Plan hours for digging.

5. Hidden Harbor

Squirrel Hill • 1708 Shady Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15217

Tiki bar with impressive tropical cocktails and themed nights. Elaborate rum creations in custom vessels.

6. Pigeon Bagels

Squirrel Hill • 2019 Murray Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15217

The best bagels in Pittsburgh. Hand-rolled, boiled, and baked fresh daily with satisfying chew.

7. Kiin Lao and Thai Eatery

Squirrel Hill • 2003 Murray Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15217

Thai classics meet bold Lao flavors with genuine heat and complex spice profiles.

8. S.W. Randall Toyes

Squirrel Hill • 2025 Murray Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15217

Independent toy store with curated quality toys, games, and books. The antidote to big-box retail.

9. Independent Brewing Co.

Squirrel Hill • 1704 Shady Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15217

Neighborhood taproom with Pittsburgh and regional craft beers. Relaxed atmosphere and knowledgeable staff.

10. Dobra Tea

Squirrel Hill • 2108 Murray Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15217

Over 100 teas from around the world in a serene, globally-inspired space. Meditation in a cup.

11. Commonplace Coffee

Squirrel Hill • 2000 Murray Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15217

Bright, modern cafe from Pittsburgh's longest-running specialty roaster. Neighborhood gathering spot.

12. Murray Avenue District

Squirrel Hill • Murray Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15217

Walkable stretch of independent businesses, international restaurants, and shops reflecting the neighborhood's diversity.
