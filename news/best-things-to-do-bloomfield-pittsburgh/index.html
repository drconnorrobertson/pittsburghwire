Best Things to Do in Bloomfield

The 12 best things to do in Bloomfield, Pittsburgh's Little Italy. Restaurants, bakeries, bars, and shops on Liberty Avenue.

By The Pittsburgh Wire May 5, 2026 6 min read

Bloomfield has been Pittsburgh's Little Italy since the early 20th century, and while the neighborhood has diversified significantly, its Italian-American heart still beats strong. Liberty Avenue serves as the commercial spine, lined with Italian delis, Vietnamese restaurants, craft breweries, and indie shops. These twelve spots capture what makes Bloomfield essential.

1. Apteka

Bloomfield • 4606 Penn Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

Nationally recognized Eastern European-inspired vegan restaurant. Featured in Bon Appetit and the New York Times.

2. Trace Brewing

Bloomfield • 4312 Main St, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

Craft brewery in a converted brass foundry. Cafe by day, brewery by night, with dog-friendly patio.

3. Tram's Kitchen

Bloomfield • 4050 Penn Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

The best Vietnamese food in the city. Hole-in-the-wall with flavor-packed pho and generous portions.

4. Bloomfield Saturday Market

Bloomfield • Liberty Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

One of Pittsburgh's largest farmer's markets with local farmers, bakers, and artisans.

5. Donatelli's Italian Food Center

Bloomfield • 4721 Liberty Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

Family-run Italian grocery with fresh pasta, imported cheeses, and house-made sauces.

6. Commonplace Coffee

Bloomfield • 4524 Liberty Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

The original location of Pittsburgh's specialty roaster. Community gathering spot since 2003.

7. Station

Bloomfield • 4744 Liberty Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

Creative comfort food with cult-following garlicky spicy wings and rotating seasonal menu.

8. Tessaro's

Bloomfield • 4601 Liberty Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

Fresh-ground Angus beef grilled over hardwood charcoal. Pittsburgh's best burger for decades.

9. Chantal's Cheese Shop

Bloomfield • 4832 Liberty Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

Artisanal cheese shop with curated domestic and imported selection, charcuterie, and custom boards.

10. Paddy Cake Bakery

Bloomfield • 4710 Liberty Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

Italian-American bakery with fresh cookies, pastries, cakes, and bread. Heritage recipes passed down generations.

11. Fet-Fisk

Bloomfield • 4407 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Scandinavian-inspired with pickled fish, Nordic small plates, and aquavit cocktails. Unique in Pittsburgh.

12. Little Italy Days

Bloomfield • Liberty Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

Annual August festival with food, music, bocce tournament, and pizza competition. Free, four days, block-party energy.
