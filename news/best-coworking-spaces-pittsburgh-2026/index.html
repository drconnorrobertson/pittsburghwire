Best Coworking Spaces in Pittsburgh

The 10 best coworking spaces in Pittsburgh for 2026. Flexible offices, startup incubators, and creative workspaces from downtown to Lawrenceville.

By The Pittsburgh Wire May 5, 2026 6 min read

Pittsburgh ranked ninth in the nation for coworking density in 2025, and the options keep expanding. Whether you are a solo freelancer needing a reliable desk, a scaling startup looking for flexible office space, or a creative professional seeking community, the city offers workspaces for every style and budget. These ten coworking spaces represent the best environments for getting work done in Pittsburgh.

1. Workbox at PPG Place

Downtown • 1 PPG Place, 31st Floor, Pittsburgh, PA 15222

Opened in early 2025 on the 31st floor of PPG Place, delivering 23,000 square feet of premium workspace with floor-to-ceiling city views. Amenities include a fitness center, private shuttle, full kitchen, phone booths, and lounge areas.

2. Ascender

East Liberty • 6023 Penn Circle S, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

More than just desk space, Ascender blends open coworking with structured business support programs including incubator bootcamps, mini-grants, and equity-focused programs for Black and Latinx founders.

3. Beauty Shoppe

Lawrenceville • 5041 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Coworking in beautifully restored historic buildings with 24/7 access to multiple locations. Memberships start at $125/month including wifi, printing, and coffee.

4. Field Day

Garfield • 113 N Pacific Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

A hybrid coffee shop and flexible workspace with a rooftop deck. Ideal for designers, nonprofit professionals, and creatives who want a beautiful workspace in a walkable neighborhood.

5. Catapult PGH

Lawrenceville • 5411 Penn Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

A no-frills coworking space offering 24/7 access, free parking, beer, coffee, a conference room with projector, and whiteboards. Affordable and reliable.

6. Alloy 26

North Side • 100 South Commons, Pittsburgh, PA 15212

Part of the Nova Place campus, offering modern open plans, private offices, and event space with easy downtown access via the T and bike trails.

7. Spaces Pittsburgh

Downtown • 525 William Penn Place, Pittsburgh, PA 15219

A premium coworking brand in the historic Oliver Building. Private offices, dedicated desks, and hot desking with a professional atmosphere suited for client-facing businesses.

8. Industrious Pittsburgh

Downtown • 11 Stanwix St, Pittsburgh, PA 15222

Fully furnished private offices and coworking memberships with a hospitality-first approach. Professional environment with wellness rooms and high-end finishes.

9. The Assembly

Strip District • 2501 Smallman St, Pittsburgh, PA 15222

A collaborative workspace in the Strip District catering to creative professionals, startups, and small teams with flexible month-to-month plans and community events.

10. Hound Haus

East Liberty • 6314 Broad St, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

A 10,000-square-foot coffee shop, bar, and coworking space with an attached dog park. The perfect spot for pet owners who work remotely.
