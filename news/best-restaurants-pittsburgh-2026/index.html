Best Restaurants in Pittsburgh 2026

Discover the 12 best restaurants in Pittsburgh for 2026, from fine dining to neighborhood gems across Lawrenceville, Strip District, and beyond.

By The Pittsburgh Wire May 5, 2026 7 min read

Pittsburgh's restaurant scene has never been stronger. From James Beard-nominated kitchens to beloved neighborhood institutions, the city's dining landscape reflects its creative spirit and deep cultural roots. Whether you are craving inventive Italian in the Strip District, refined French cuisine in Lawrenceville, or boundary-pushing vegan fare in Bloomfield, these twelve restaurants represent the very best Pittsburgh has to offer in 2026.

1. Poulet Bleu

Lawrenceville • 5104 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

After a triumphant return from a three-year renovation, Poulet Bleu remains Pittsburgh's premier French dining destination. Chef Justin Severino's menu celebrates classic technique with seasonal ingredients, and the bar program is among the city's most ambitious. The reimagined interior is stunning, and the duck confit alone is worth the trip.

2. DiAnoia's Eatery

Strip District • 2549 Penn Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15222

Dave Anoia's modern Italian kitchen is a Strip District anchor. House-made pastas, wood-fired dishes, and a weekend brunch that draws lines around the block define this energetic space. The rigatoni with Sunday gravy is legendary, and the cocktail list leans creative without losing its Italian soul.

3. Apteka

Bloomfield • 4606 Penn Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

This Eastern European-inspired vegan restaurant and bar has earned national recognition for proving that plant-based cuisine can be inventive, satisfying, and deeply flavorful. The pierogi and seasonal beet dishes are transformative, and the natural wine list is one of the best in the city.

4. Fet-Fisk

Bloomfield • 4407 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Scandinavian-inspired and utterly unique in Pittsburgh's dining scene, Fet-Fisk brings pickled fish, house-cured meats, and Nordic-influenced small plates to a warm, intimate setting. The smoked fish board and aquavit cocktails transport you to coastal Sweden.

5. Morcilla

Lawrenceville • 3519 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

A Spanish-inspired sibling to the acclaimed Cure, Morcilla specializes in pintxos, charcuterie, and bold Iberian flavors. The morcilla sausage and patatas bravas are essential orders, and the sherry selection is unmatched in Pittsburgh.

6. The Parlor Dim Sum

Lawrenceville • 5168 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Cantonese dim sum meets creative cocktails at this Lawrenceville favorite. The har gow, siu mai, and char siu bao are executed with precision, while the inventive drink menu adds a modern edge that makes this a standout in the city's Asian dining scene.

7. Dish Osteria and Bar

South Side • 128 S 17th St, Pittsburgh, PA 15203

Widely considered one of the best Italian restaurants in Pittsburgh, Dish Osteria has been a South Side institution for over a decade. The handmade pastas, wood-fired pizzas, and thoughtful wine list create an experience that feels both authentic and distinctly Pittsburgh.

8. Altius

Mount Washington • 1230 Grandview Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15211

Fine dining with a billion-dollar view. Altius pairs contemporary American cuisine with panoramic vistas of the Pittsburgh skyline from Mount Washington. Chef Jessica Bauer's tasting menus showcase seasonal Pennsylvania ingredients at their absolute peak.

9. Casbah

East Liberty • 229 S Highland Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

A neighborhood restaurant and wine bar offering northern Mediterranean-inspired cuisine from Italy, Spain, France, and Greece. The year-round garden patio, local ingredient focus, and approachable elegance have made Casbah an East Liberty mainstay for decades.

10. Pusadee's Garden

Lawrenceville • 5321 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Authentic Thai cuisine served in a gorgeous garden setting. Pusadee's brings the flavors of northern Thailand to Pittsburgh with dishes like khao soi, green curry, and pad thai that taste nothing like their Americanized counterparts. The outdoor patio is magical in summer.

11. Cure

Lawrenceville • 5336 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

Chef Justin Severino's charcuterie-focused restaurant redefined what fine dining means in Pittsburgh. House-cured meats, inventive small plates, and an exceptional natural wine program make every visit feel like an event. The tasting menu is a masterclass in craft.

12. Superior Motors

Braddock • 1211 Braddock Ave, Braddock, PA 15104

Located in a converted car dealership in Braddock, Superior Motors blends fine dining with community mission. Chef Kevin Sousa's prix fixe menus spotlight hyper-local ingredients while supporting neighborhood revitalization. It is dining with purpose and extraordinary flavor.
