Best Things to Do in Shadyside

The 12 best things to do in Shadyside, Pittsburgh. Shopping on Walnut Street, dining, parks, and culture in the city's most elegant neighborhood.

By The Pittsburgh Wire May 5, 2026 6 min read

Shadyside is Pittsburgh at its most polished. Tree-lined streets, Victorian mansions, and two distinct commercial corridors along Walnut Street and Ellsworth Avenue create a neighborhood blending upscale shopping with genuine warmth. These twelve spots capture the best of Shadyside.

1. Girasole

Shadyside • 733 Copeland St, Pittsburgh, PA 15232

Critically acclaimed Italian with refined northern cuisine in an intimate, candlelit setting. Handmade pastas and curated wines.

2. Walnut Street Shopping

Shadyside • Walnut St, Pittsburgh, PA 15232

Pittsburgh's premier shopping with national retailers and independent boutiques. Best walkable shopping experience in the city.

3. Noodlehead

Shadyside • 5907 Penn Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

Crowd favorite for Southeast Asian street food. BYOB policy keeps evenings affordable. Expect a line that is always worth it.

4. Mercurio's

Shadyside • 5523 Walnut St, Pittsburgh, PA 15232

Wood-fired Italian pizza and homemade gelato on Walnut Street. Sidewalk seating is prime people-watching territory.

5. Mellon Park

Shadyside • 1047 Shady Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15232

Historic park with walled garden, tennis courts, and shaded paths. One of the most photographed spots in the city.

6. Whitehouse Salon

Shadyside • 5523 Walnut St, Pittsburgh, PA 15232

Fashion-forward salon in Shadyside's style district. Quality work matching the neighborhood's polished aesthetic.

7. Cafe Moulin

Shadyside • 227 N Highland Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

Authentic French cafe with sweet and savory crepes, pastries, and excellent coffee in an intimate space.

8. Kards Unlimited

Shadyside • 5522 Walnut St, Pittsburgh, PA 15232

Quirky gift shop packed with cards, books, toys, and oddities. An hour of browsing always yields something unexpected.

9. Ellsworth Avenue

Shadyside • Ellsworth Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15232

Art galleries, antique shops, restaurants, and wine bars. Slower pace with more personal discoveries than the main drag.

10. The Yard Gastropub

Shadyside • 5860 Ellsworth Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15232

Creative grilled cheese sandwiches and craft beer. Casual comfort-food focus with the legendary truffle grilled cheese.

11. Pittsburgh Center for the Arts

Shadyside • 6300 Fifth Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15232

Community arts center with exhibitions, classes in film, photography, ceramics, and fine arts.

12. Hey Betty!

Shadyside • 5816 Ellsworth Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15232

Vintage and consignment clothing with curated secondhand fashion at fair prices. Sustainable style.
