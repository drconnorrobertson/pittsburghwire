Best Coffee Shops in Pittsburgh

The 12 best coffee shops in Pittsburgh for 2026. From specialty roasters to cozy neighborhood cafes across the city.

By The Pittsburgh Wire May 5, 2026 6 min read

Pittsburgh's specialty coffee scene has quietly become one of the best in the Northeast. Local roasters are building direct trade relationships with farms around the world, and neighborhood cafes are doubling as creative hubs and community gathering places. Whether you need a perfectly pulled espresso, a pour-over with single-origin beans, or just a beautiful space to work for the afternoon, these twelve coffee shops represent the best of what Pittsburgh has to offer.

1. De Fer Coffee & Tea

Strip District • 1118 Smallman St, Pittsburgh, PA 15222

This employee-owned roastery and cafe in the Strip District is Pittsburgh's specialty coffee standard-bearer. Founded in 2017, De Fer roasts in small batches on-site and serves creative drinks like espresso tonics and cherry blossom lattes alongside expertly brewed classics.

2. Commonplace Coffee

Multiple Locations • 4524 Liberty Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

Pittsburgh's longest-running specialty roaster, founded in 2003, with locations in Bloomfield, Squirrel Hill, and East Liberty. Commonplace emphasizes direct trade relationships and small-batch roasting with seasonal single-origin offerings that are consistently excellent.

3. Espresso a Mano

Lawrenceville • 3623 Butler St, Pittsburgh, PA 15201

A Lawrenceville institution known for some of the most impressive latte art in the city. Espresso a Mano focuses exclusively on the craft of espresso, offering a tight menu of beautifully prepared drinks in a cozy, unpretentious space.

4. Redstart Roasters

East End • 4024 Penn Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

A sustainable, bird-friendly microroaster that donates a portion of every sale to bird preservation. Beyond the mission, the coffee is exceptional. Redstart sources unique lots and roasts them to highlight origin character.

5. KLVN Coffee Lab

East Liberty • 6029 Penn Circle S, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

A specialty roaster and cafe with a scientific approach to coffee. KLVN sources exceptional green coffees and roasts with precision, offering rotating single-origins as pour-over and espresso in a modern East Liberty cafe.

6. Allegheny Coffee & Tea Exchange

Strip District • 2121 Penn Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15222

A Strip District landmark since 1985, this shop roasts coffee in-house and stocks an enormous selection of loose-leaf teas. The aroma hits you from half a block away. A no-frills, quality-first operation caffeinating Pittsburgh for four decades.

7. Gasoline Street Coffee

Downtown • 437 Grant St, Pittsburgh, PA 15219

A downtown cafe with cool industrial-retro vibes, serving beans from local roaster Commonplace Coffee. The space is ideal for a quick meeting or focused work session, with exposed brick, vintage signage, and consistently well-made espresso drinks.

8. Shibam Coffee Co.

North Oakland • 3622 Forbes Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15213

Specializing in Yemeni coffee with traditional preparations featuring cardamom, ginger, and cinnamon. Shibam brings a completely unique coffee experience to Pittsburgh, honoring centuries-old Yemeni brewing traditions in a welcoming modern space.

9. Convive Coffee

Bloomfield • 4517 Liberty Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

A Bloomfield newcomer that has quickly earned a devoted following. Convive pairs excellent specialty coffee with a small menu of pastries and light bites in a bright, airy, minimalist space.

10. Arriviste Coffee

Friendship • 4621 Friendship Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

Tucked into the quiet Friendship neighborhood, Arriviste offers exceptional pour-overs and a curated selection of beans from top roasters. The intimate space feels like a well-kept secret.

11. La Prima Espresso

Strip District • 205 21st St, Pittsburgh, PA 15222

A Strip District institution roasting Italian-style espresso since 1988. La Prima supplies dozens of Pittsburgh cafes and restaurants, but the best experience is at the source, where you can watch beans roast while sipping a perfect cappuccino.

12. Trace Brewing Coffee

Bloomfield • 4312 Main St, Pittsburgh, PA 15224

Housed in an old brass and bronze foundry, Trace operates as a cafe by day and brewery by night. The morning coffee program features local roasters, excellent pastries, and a dog-friendly patio perfect for weekend mornings.
