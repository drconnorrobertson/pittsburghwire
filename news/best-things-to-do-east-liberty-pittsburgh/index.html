Best Things to Do in East Liberty

The 12 best things to do in East Liberty, Pittsburgh. Restaurants, nightlife, shopping, and culture in one of the city's fastest-growing neighborhoods.

By The Pittsburgh Wire May 5, 2026 6 min read

East Liberty has undergone one of the most dramatic transformations in Pittsburgh. What was once a struggling commercial district is now a thriving hub of restaurants, bars, creative businesses, and tech companies. From cutting-edge cocktail bars to beloved institutions, these twelve spots represent the best of East Liberty today.

1. Paris 66

East Liberty • 6018 Centre Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

Authentic French restaurant bringing Parisian brunch culture to Pittsburgh. Champagne, croissants, and pastries.

2. Casbah

East Liberty • 229 S Highland Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

Mediterranean-inspired cuisine with year-round garden patio and seasonal local menus.

3. Lorelei

East Liberty • 124 S Highland Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

Moody cocktail bar with creative drinks and vintage aesthetic. One of Pittsburgh's best bars.

4. KLVN Coffee Lab

East Liberty • 6029 Penn Circle S, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

Specialty roaster with scientific approach and modern cafe that is a neighborhood anchor.

5. The Forge

East Liberty • 6025 Penn Circle S, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

Sleek restaurant with industrial-chic interior, creative menu, and top cocktail program.

6. Bakery Square

East Liberty • 6425 Penn Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

Mixed-use development on former Nabisco site housing Google, shops, and restaurants.

7. Trader Joe's

East Liberty • 6343 Penn Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

Beloved grocery store drawing shoppers citywide for affordable wines, frozen favorites, and specialty items.

8. Hey Babe

East Liberty • 622 N Highland Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

Craft cocktail lounge in historic YMCA building. Upscale-yet-approachable with inventive drinks.

9. Hound Haus

East Liberty • 6314 Broad St, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

Coffee shop, bar, and coworking with a dog park. Unique concept for remote-working pet owners.

10. Tous les Jours

East Liberty • 5972 Centre Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

Korean bakery with fresh bread, elaborate cakes, and cream-filled pastries new to Pittsburgh.

11. Condado Tacos

East Liberty • 135 Bakery Square Blvd, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

Build-your-own tacos with creative shells, inventive fillings, and tequila-forward bar.

12. Carnegie Library

East Liberty • 130 S Whitfield St, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

Beautifully renovated branch serving as community hub with gorgeous reading room and free programming.
